KORUZA

KORUZA brings benefits of fibre-optic connection, without the intrusive installation process or the high price tag. It is a license-free, wireless optical internet-access system with data securely transmitted point-to-point over a collimated beam of light with 1 Gbps or 10 Gbps throughput at distances up to 150 m.
