IoT node development

IoT devices enable power-efficeint sensing in a wide range of application. Rapidly designing, manufacturing and deploying a well-tailored solution empowers our customers to capture their market rapidly and deliver a next-generator service.

We integrate the latest LoraWAN, WiFi and BLE modules with our lates power management solutions and if required solar and other power sources to enable long term operation. We develop and manufacture the nodes following our collaborative design process, from proof-of-concept devices to a batch of devices ready to deploy within weeks. Our unique combination of multi-disciplinary engineering experts and in-house manufacturing allows us to create custom products within a few weeks.

IoT Edge Computing Machine Learning

We specialize in developing off-grid edge-computing IoT devices capable of processing sensor information, video and images to create compact answers, rapid responses to changes in environemnt and low bandwidth to be suitable for communicating at long distances via LoraWAN, satellite modems and other long-range connections from any location in the world.

Implementing the lastest single-board computers from Raspberry Pi and combining them with well proven open-source processing packages we implement solutions ranging from visually monitoring an analog meter that can not be replaced in a factory basement to observing penguin migrations in Antarctica.

USE CASES

Sea turtle tracking

Working with nature conservation specialists at Arribada Initiative and ZSL London we have developed a rugged IoT sea turtle tag capable of taking up to 10000 photos or 1200 minutes of video at depths of up to 500m and respond via LoraWAN when on surface, empowering better research in habitats and environmental protection.

Air quality and radiation

Collaborating with the worlds largest citizen science grroups for radiation monitoring Safecast we have developed an off-grid standalone radiation and air-quality sensor Solarcast to be deployed in remote locations, forrest fire reagions and nuclear exclusion zones and provide real-time data on the environemnt.

Penguin monitoring

Observing animal behaviour in remote areas exploses global warming effects and changes to the environment. We have developed a ruggest system for Antarctica to be used by Penguin Watch and Arribada Initiative for annual penguin mingration and nesting monitoring, operating completely off-grid in one of the worlds coldest and most remote areas.

IoT drone mapping

IoT devices must often be deployed in hidden or remote locations not optimal for wirelss communication. We have developed an automated drone RF mapping solution, enabling our customers and ourselves to evaluate the performance of their gateway, exactly determine the coverage, understand the effect of the environment on signal coverage as well as rapidly test potential gateway locations prior to prematurely investing in infrastructure.

Drone mapping of LoraWAN coverage empoweres us during on-site deployments of IoT devices to rapidly determine the best scenarios, say determining gateway locations on Principe island for Arribada PitStop turtle tagging project or optimizing agricultural sensor positioning in Slovenia.

We offer the drone mapping technology and expertese to our customers, customized development of mapping hardware, algorithms and vizualizations as well as on-site support and training.

LoraWAN drone mapping

3D gateway coverage

Determine the coverage of LoraWAN gateway in un-accessible locations and perform repeatable measurements durign optimization process. Observe the coverage over water, in tree canopy, along the edge of the forrest or on top of lighting infrastructure.

Antenna characterization in real-world deployments

Radiation patterns of antennas are significantly altered by surrounding objects and mounting structures. By deploying a LoraWAN gateway and 3D mapping coverage in its vicinty, such effects can be rapidly observed, adjusted for and changes verified.

Gateway placement optimization

IoT deployments in remote locations with lack of built infrastructure typically present a challenge for connectivity. We have pioneered the approach of deploying IoT nodes first and assisted with the drone evaluated the optimal position for its placement. This allows one to quickly determine which corner for a tall building will result in best coverage, which tall tree is most suitable for the gateway and evalaute many other use-case.
